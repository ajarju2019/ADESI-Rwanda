About ADESI – Rwanda

We connect education, technology, sustainability, and community development to drive measurable impact.

Mission

To empower communities in Rwanda through inclusive education, digital skills development, research-driven innovation, and sustainable development initiatives.

Vision

A Rwanda where youth and women have equitable access to quality education, digital opportunities, and sustainable solutions that enable inclusive growth and resilience.

Core Values

  • Inclusivity & Equity – ensuring access for underserved groups
  • Innovation & Learning – evidence-based, adaptive solutions
  • Sustainability – climate resilience and long-term impact
  • Integrity & Accountability – transparency and ethical practice
  • Community Ownership – participatory, locally led development
  • Partnership & Collaboration – collective impact with stakeholders
