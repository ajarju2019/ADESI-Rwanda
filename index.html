African Digital Education & Sustainability Initiative

Empowering Rwanda through Digital Skills & Sustainable Development

ADESI operates at the intersection of education, technology, sustainability, and community development, with a strong focus on youth, women, educators, and underserved communities.

Explore Programs Support Our Work

Mission

To empower communities in Rwanda through inclusive education, digital skills development, research-driven innovation, and sustainable development initiatives.

Vision

A Rwanda where youth and women have equitable access to quality education, digital opportunities, and sustainable solutions that enable inclusive growth and resilience.

Strategic Plan

2025–2028: Digital Education • Empowerment • Research & Innovation • Climate Action • Partnerships
